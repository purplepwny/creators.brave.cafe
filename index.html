This webring is a place for Brave creators to share their work and promote others in the Brave ecosytem. We welcomes unique websites, content, and portfolios. To join, submit a pull request or follow these steps:

  1. Join the Brave Creator program and add your website.
  2. Add a link to https://creators.brave.cafe/ on the home page, header, or footer of your website.
  3. Links should take the form of a clearly visible text link (e.g., "Brave Creator Webring"), our light, or dark icons.
  4. E-mail brave-admin@pbanks.net with a link to your website and a few words of description.
